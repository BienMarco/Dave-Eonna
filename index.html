Our Story

It began in the soft glow of high school days, when she came to me—not for love, but to speak of another who sought her heart. Fate has a quiet sense of humor—for though I knew his name, it was hers I would never forget.

She was talkative, full of energy and life. I was the calm one—a listener, drawn to the warmth of her voice. Somehow, we just fit—two opposites dancing in perfect rhythm.

As time passed, something deeper began to grow between us. A feeling that started as a spark soon rooted itself quietly in my heart… and in hers too.

When I finally found the courage to tell her how I felt, she welcomed it with open arms and a joyful heart. That was the beginning of a new chapter—one filled with love, laughter, and shared dreams.

Now, after ten beautiful years together, we stand once more on the edge of something new. Another chapter is glancing our way, and I know, with all my heart, I want to keep writing this story—with her, forever.
